About GirlHotels

Empowering women travelers with verified, trusted accommodations that prioritize safety, comfort, and unique needs.

Our Mission

Safe Stays. Empowered Travels.

GirlHotels was founded with a simple belief: every woman deserves to travel with confidence. Whether you're embarking on your first solo adventure, planning a girlfriend getaway, or traveling for business, you shouldn't have to compromise on safety or comfort.

We've built the world's most comprehensive directory of female-friendly hotels, rigorously verified and rated specifically for women travelers. Every property in our directory meets our strict safety standards and has been evaluated from a woman's perspective.
