What I Learned About Traveling Solo

The Logistics Matter Less Than You Think

I had anxiety about safety, about navigating alone, about looking like a lost tourist. But Paris is incredibly manageable. The Metro is intuitive. English is widely spoken but French efforts are appreciated. I never felt unsafe‚Äîeven walking home alone at midnight through the Marais.

The Real Luxury Is Your Own Schedule
