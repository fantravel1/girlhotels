Solo Safety Tips

  • Bikes are king: Rent a bike and explore like a local. Just stay in the bike lanes!
  • Night walking: Amsterdam is very safe at night. The canals are well-lit and there's always foot traffic.
  • Red Light District: It's tourist-friendly and safe to walk through, even alone. Just respect the workers (no photos!).
  • Pickpockets: Watch your bag on trains and in crowded areas. Otherwise, crime is low.
