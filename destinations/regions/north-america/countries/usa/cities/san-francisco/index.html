The Tenderloin: Bordered by Union Square, this area has visible homelessness, open drug use, and safety concerns especially after dark. While some excellent restaurants are here, solo women should exercise caution or avoid entirely. If your hotel is near this area, use ride apps at night.

Mark Twain's quote about cold summers is true. Pack layers even in July. The fog burns off by noon in most areas. East Bay and South Bay are sunnier if you're day-tripping.

SF is exceptionally solo-friendly. Bar seating at restaurants is standard and often preferred. The Ferry Building, Tartine, and countless counters welcome single diners. Coffee culture is huge.
