Safety Tips for Women in CDMX

üöó Transport

Always use Uber, Didi, or InDriver‚Äînever street taxis. The metro is generally safe during the day; women-only cars are available during rush hour. Walking is safe in recommended neighborhoods during daylight.

üì± Stay Connected

Get a local SIM or use eSIM for data. Share your location with someone back home. Keep your phone charged‚Äîit's your lifeline for navigation and ride-hailing.

üí∞ Money & Belongings

Don't flash expensive jewelry or electronics. Use a crossbody bag in crowded areas. Carry small bills and avoid pulling out large amounts of cash. ATMs inside banks are safest.

üåô Nightlife

Stick to Roma, Condesa, and Polanco for nightlife. Watch your drinks. Uber home rather than walking late at night. Trust your instincts‚Äîif somewhere feels off, leave.
