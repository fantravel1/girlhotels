Florence Solo Travel Tips

üéüÔ∏è Book Museum Tickets Ahead

The Uffizi and Accademia have long lines. Book timed tickets online to avoid standing outside. Early morning or late afternoon slots are best.

üëú Watch for Pickpockets

Florence is very safe, but pickpockets target tourists at busy sites. Keep valuables secure at the Duomo, San Lorenzo market, and on buses.

üç∑ Solo Aperitivo Culture

Italians love aperitivo (pre-dinner drinks with snacks). It's completely normal to enjoy alone at a bar counter. Santo Spirito piazza is perfect for people-watching.

üëü Walking City

Florence is compact and best explored on foot. Wear comfortable shoes‚Äîstreets are cobblestone. The centro storico is car-free during the day.
