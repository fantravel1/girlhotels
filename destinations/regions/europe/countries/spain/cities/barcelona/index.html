Barcelona Safety Tips

üëõ Pickpocket Prevention

Barcelona has high pickpocket activity on La Rambla, in the metro, and at major tourist sites. Use a crossbody bag, keep valuables in front pockets, and be especially alert in crowds.

üåô Night Safety

Main areas are safe until late. Avoid poorly lit streets in El Raval and Barceloneta beach after midnight. Uber and Cabify work well here. The metro runs until midnight (2am Fri/Sat).

üèñÔ∏è Beach Tips

Never leave belongings unattended at the beach‚Äîthefts are common. Avoid accepting drinks from strangers. The chiringuitos (beach bars) are generally safe and fun.

üç∑ Dining Solo

Barcelona is great for solo dining. Bar seating at tapas places is perfect for solo travelers. Eating late (9-10pm dinner) is normal‚Äîyou won't look out of place.
