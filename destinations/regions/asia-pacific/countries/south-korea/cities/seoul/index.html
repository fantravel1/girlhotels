Seoul Tips for Solo Women

Seoul's subway is immaculate, safe, and runs until midnight. Women-only cars exist on some lines during rush hour (marked in pink). Use the Kakao Metro app for English navigation.

ğŸ— Solo Dining

Korea has embraced "honbap" (eating alone) culture. Many restaurants have counter seating. Convenience stores (CU, GS25) have excellent hot food. Chimaek (chicken + beer) is perfectly acceptable solo.
